PDF Text to Speech

Welcome! Let us set you up in 10 seconds.

Most important step: Pin the extension

  1. Click the Extensions icon (puzzle piece) in Chrome.
  2. Find PDF Text to Speech.
  3. Click the pin icon to keep it on the toolbar.
Tip: Pinning gives you one-click access on every PDF.

How to use

  1. Open any PDF in Chrome.
  2. Click the extension icon.
  3. Press Read Aloud.

If a PDF is inside Gmail, use "Open in new window" or download it and open with the extension.
