PDF Text to Speech Installed

Click the puzzle piece (1) in the top right of your browser. Then, click the little pin (2) next to the extension.

Open the extension (3) on any page to start using PDF Text to Speech:

How to use

Open a new tab, then upload a PDF in the extension.

  1. Click the extension icon (1).
  2. Click Open PDF File (2).
  3. Press Read Aloud (3).

If a PDF is inside Gmail, use "Open in new window" or download it and open it with the extension.
