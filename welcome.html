Welcome! Let us set you up in 10 seconds.

Most important step: Pin the extension

Pin the extension so it stays visible in the toolbar.

PDF Text to Speech
  1. Click the Extensions icon (1).
  2. Find PDF Text to Speech.
  3. Click the pin icon (2).
Tip: Pinning gives you one-click access on every PDF.

How to use

Open a new tab, then upload a PDF in the extension.

  1. Open a new tab in Chrome.
  2. Click the extension icon (1).
  3. Click Open PDF File (2).
  4. Press Read Aloud.

If a PDF is inside Gmail, use "Open in new window" or download it and open it with the extension.
